Humanities

Course Description

Humanities is a course that covers a broad number of topics, from Ancient Civilizations like Mesopotamia to current day. It acts mainly as an English/History class with other miscellaneous topics thrown in there, like music theory. This course is taught by Mrs. Kristen Small.


A Term

The A Term was almost solely focused on Thoreau's Walden, which was our summer reading assignment for this course. Our first project was implemented on the second day of school; it entailed the drafting of a skit based on Thoreau's Walden and the performance of that skit at Camp Bournedale.
