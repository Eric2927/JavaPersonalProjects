Science Technical Writing (STW)

Course Description

STW is a course that focuses on scientific writing. Its topics range from correcting grammar to deciphering science papers. This class is taught by Mrs. Siobhan Curran.


A Term

Grammar is our primary A term focus. We went through run-on sentences, parallelism, and several other concepts. We also looked at abstracts in science writing and how to read them.


Click here to see a paper we read in class as an example of what should not be done in a STEM paper.


B Term

Throughout B Term, STW went alongside STEM I. Many things we did in STW was to support our STEM I project. For instance, we focused a lot on writing certain parts of a STEM thesis so that we could do that for our STEM project later on.
